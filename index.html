About Us

Welcome to Sparky Foods and Beverages, a dynamic Indian proprietorship firm established in February 2022 by Mrs. Pushpa Singh. Our brand is dedicated to delivering a diverse range of refreshing beverages that blend tradition with innovation. From the tangy zest of Jeera Blast to the timeless refreshment of Jaipuri Shikanji, and the crisp satisfaction of our Fruit Beer and Premium Soda, we cater to every palate. Our Packaged Drinking Water ensures purity in every drop. At Sparky, we pride ourselves on quality, taste, and a passion for creating beverages that truly sparkle with the essence of India.

Vision

Our vision at Sparky Foods and Beverages is to become a leading name in the beverage industry by blending the richness of Indian traditions with innovative flavors that captivate modern consumers. We aspire to create beverages that not only refresh but also inspire, bringing people together through the joy of unique and authentic tastes. Committed to quality and sustainability, we aim to expand our presence across India and beyond, while consistently delivering products that uphold our values of purity, excellence, and customer satisfaction. Sparky envisions a future where every sip reflects our passion for creating beverages that truly stand out.
